This is me

Hello, I'm Ranphy Joel Felix De Alba, currently a student at the Inter American University of Puerto Rico, where I'm studying Video Game Design and Network Security. I have a strong passion for learning and creating content that resonates with people, which is why I aspire to join the video game industry. In this vast field, there are numerous exciting directions to explore, including storytelling, music, sound design, art, programming, and more. However, my particular interest lies in gameplay programming, especially in the realm of video game AI. I find it fascinating how AI in games can provide challenging experiences for players, whether it's through boss fights or enemy encounters. Outside the realm of programming I also enjoy deeply the design and conceptualization of worlds, ideas, characters, and concrete storytelling. There's so much to learn still and I'm always eager to keep learning.
